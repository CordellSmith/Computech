About CompuTech

CompuTech was founded by John Smithers in 2009. In John's early 30's he had a dream to start a computer business that can provide a quality of service to its customers unlike any other tech business. His dream was spearheaded in 2012 by a small team who also shared his vision in the local suburb of Fremantle, Perth WA. It didn't take long for the business to grow as positive word spread like wildfire about the quality of service that was brought to its customers. This flourish of business attracted the attention of major technology brands who backed the business, funding a major expansion to a national level. CompuTech is now located in every state in Australia with its largest retailers based out of Perth, Melbourne and Sydney.

Our Core Values

  • Deliver WOW Through Service
  • Honesty and Integrity to the Customer and Employees
  • Build a Positive Team and Family Spirit
  • Be Passionate and Determined

CompuTechs business model is developed around helping all levels of technology skilled patrons as well as helping small to medium business get the most out of their IT investment. The small Perth company of 10 employees has now expanded to nearly 100 employees providing local jobs for the community. John Smithers is a man whos dreams could only have been achieved through feeding back into the community and helping it grow and thats why 40% of all large purchases goes directly to local community charities.
